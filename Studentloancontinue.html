Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible for a no co-signer student loan?
International and domestic students admitted to eligible schools may apply without a co-signer, based on future earning potential and academic standing.
What are the benefits of applying without a co-signer?
You don't need a credit history or a guarantor – the loan decision is based on your program and expected income.
What documents do I need?
Typically your passport, acceptance letter, academic transcripts, and proof of funds for living expenses.
What are the loan repayment terms?
Repayment usually starts 6 months after graduation, with flexible terms up to 20 years depending on the loan.
Can I repay my loan early?
Yes, you can make early repayments without any prepayment penalties.
What are the benefits of choosing this loan?
Competitive interest rates, no co-signer required, and support for students from various backgrounds.
